Episode I

A new code

A junior web developer starts his journey into the universe of coding.

Hailing from a small distant planet of Coderine, our protagonist, born on a code farm, encounters an old hermit who will train him in the ways of coding. From HTML to CSS, Javascript and so much more.

But he must beware of the dark web. Random youtube video's, facebook and tumblr, lurks around the corner, all leading to the ways of procrastination.

He will have to steel his will and avoid all temptations should he wish to succeed.

Hoping to one day becoming a worthy full stack developer, he trains hard. And with some help from his peers, who knows what amazing web designs he may conjure one day.

Young coder, you're our only hope..
